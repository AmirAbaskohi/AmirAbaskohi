## About Me

I’m **Amirhossein Abaskohi**, a Computer Science PhD student at the **University of British Columbia**.

I love working on systems that can research, reason, collaborate, and act over complex information. My current interests include **deep research agents**, **computer-use agents**, **multi-agent systems**, **long-document reasoning**, **LLM interpretability**, **multimodal models**, and **world models**.
